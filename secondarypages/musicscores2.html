Bach - Violin Sonata in c minor BWV 1017


The Composition

Personal impressions

Personal history

I heard this string quartet for the first time on a set of CDs which I bought at my first job in Luxembourg, when I was about 28 years old. It was an EMI box set of the complete Beethoven string quartets played by the Alban Berg quartet. A colleague of mine, an eccentric Frenchman, who was about 37 at that time, informed me that he had never heard of them.


Form

The quartet has 4 movements: Allegro con brio (F major) Adagio affettuoso ed appassionato (D minor) Scherzo: Allegro molto (F major) Allegro (F major)
